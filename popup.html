TPCI · PERMISSION INVENTORY

What can your extensions actually do?

Sorted by what they can access — worst first. This is not a malware scan.

This tool makes no judgment about whether an extension is safe. It only reads the permissions each extension has declared, and orders them so the broadest access is easiest to spot. Whether that access makes sense is a call only you can make — you know what each extension is supposed to do. No data leaves your device. Built by The Privacy Commons Institute as a companion to our research on malicious Chrome extension persistence.
